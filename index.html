Singer-Songwriters

Who are we?

We are a collection of acoustic singer-songwriters in Saigon. We love performing, hearing new artists and organising shows. We put on weekly open mic nights and jam sessions to bring musicians from across the city together, as well as organising monthly showcases of local artists. Here you can share your own events with people who are passionate about live music, and we will professionally promote any and all events you send us for free. We're here to support all local musicians bring their music to the public and we hope to see you soon!

What's going on?

We are always putting on events in Saigon! Whether you want to get involved, or just enjoy some music, check them all out below.

Every Week

Monday

Indika, 9.00pm

Open Mic Night. For musicians of all styles and skills, all performers and music lovers welcome!

Tuesday

Yoko Cafe, 8.30pm

Open Stage. For solo artists, bands, and people just looking to jam, Yoko cafe is very welcoming of all!

Thursday

Hem Chill, 8.00pm

Open Mic Night. A laid back open mic by the river in D2. A perfect spot to show your new tunes or just vibe!

Sunday

Capone's, 8.30pm

Jam Night. A lively jam session with some of the best musicians in the city. All genres and people are welcome!

Every Month

SSS Showcase

Suma - Last Friday of the Month

This is our monthly show of musicians and bands from all over Saigon. The line-up is always varied every month, and if you want to join in, come down to our weekly events and talk to the organisers!

Rising Stars

Hem Chill - 2nd Friday of the Month

This is our show of new talent in the city. If you're looking to start performing regularly in the city, this is perfect for you! Performers get 30 minute sets to show their music and get used to performing live, but make sure you arrive early to get a spot!
